---
description: Run formal governance audit with weighted scorecard
---

1. Load audit template from `.ai-governance/templates/audit-scorecard.md`
2. Score each of the 8 audit dimensions (0-10)
3. Compute weighted overall score
4. Determine verdict: PASS (≥85%), CONDITIONAL (70-84%), FAIL (<70%)
5. Document critical issues, warnings, and observations
6. Generate required actions list
7. Present scorecard for human sign-off
